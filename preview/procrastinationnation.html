Our beliefs

What is procrastination? Are we against it or for it?

What is procrastination?

According to a quick Google search, procrastination is the "action of delaying or postponing something". While that is true, it's not all about the postponing it; we like to do everything last minute, under panic. Some say they even work better under panic, because of the adrenaline released.

Do we believe in following the "art of procrastination"?

First, procrastination is natural. We want to put off the hard stuff for last because we don't want to do it. We'd rather either do the easy stuff or nothing at all. Because of it being natural, it's hard to fight it, we don't want people really wasting* time on trying to stop procrastination, but we have seen and experienced the effects of procrastination. It's not pretty.

* Some people may not agree with our word choice, however, fighting procrastination takes a lot of time.
